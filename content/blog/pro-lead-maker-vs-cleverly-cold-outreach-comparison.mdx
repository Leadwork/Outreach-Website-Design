If you're shopping for a cold outreach agency, two names you'll likely run into are **Cleverly** and **Pro Lead Maker**. Both run done-for-you B2B outreach. Both work with founders, SaaS, and agencies. But they're built differently — and the right pick depends on your channel priority, budget, and how hands-off you want to be.

This is an honest comparison. We're Pro Lead Maker, so we have a side, but we'll be straight about where Cleverly is the better fit. Picking the wrong agency wastes months, so it's in nobody's interest to oversell.

## The 30-second answer

- **Choose Cleverly if** you want a large, established US-based brand with a big LinkedIn footprint, a self-serve dashboard, and you're comfortable with templated workflows across thousands of clients.
- **Choose Pro Lead Maker if** you want a small senior team running your campaigns hands-on, true multichannel (email + LinkedIn) included in one package, and noticeably lower pricing for the same scope.

## Channels: where each one is strongest

Cleverly built its reputation on **LinkedIn outreach** — it's their core, and they're good at it. Email came later as a supplement. If LinkedIn is 80% of your strategy and you want the agency with the deepest LinkedIn-specific track record, that's a real point in Cleverly's favor.

Pro Lead Maker treats **email and LinkedIn as equal halves of one motion**. Our Done-For-You package runs both channels together — a prospect sees your email and your LinkedIn message in a coordinated sequence, which lifts reply rates versus either channel alone. If you want true multichannel in a single package rather than stacked add-ons, that's where we fit.

## Pricing: the honest breakdown

Cleverly's published entry point is around $397/month for LinkedIn, scaling up as you add email, dedicated SDRs, and volume. Full-service plans climb into the several-thousand-per-month range, in line with most US agencies.

Pro Lead Maker prices lower across the board because our team is based in Bangladesh — same senior operators, lower overhead. Our full multichannel Done-For-You package (5 sending domains, 15 inboxes, full infrastructure, 2,000 verified contacts/month, email + LinkedIn sequences, reply handling) is **$1,000/month with no setup fee**. For many teams that's less than a single-channel plan elsewhere.

Lower price isn't automatically better — if you specifically want US-based account managers in your timezone, that's a legitimate reason to pay more. We're upfront about that trade-off.

## Team model: big brand vs senior boutique

This is the real philosophical difference.

**Cleverly** is a large operation serving over 10,000 clients. That scale brings battle-tested playbooks and a polished dashboard — but it also means more templating and a higher client-to-manager ratio.

**Pro Lead Maker** is an 8-person specialist team with a much smaller client book. The founder (MD. Al Amin) is on every account. You get more bespoke copy and faster, more personal communication — but we're a boutique, not a 100-person agency, and we're honest about that.

## What neither of us will promise

Here's something worth checking with **any** agency you evaluate: be skeptical of guaranteed meeting numbers. We don't promise "X meetings per month" because meeting volume depends on your offer, deal size, market timing, and ICP fit — factors no agency fully controls. What a good agency *can* guarantee is the system, daily operations, transparent reporting, and quality control on every reply. If an agency leads with a guaranteed number, ask exactly what happens when they miss it.

## Which should you choose?

**Pick Cleverly if:**

- LinkedIn is the overwhelming majority of your strategy
- You want a large, well-known US brand
- A self-serve dashboard matters to you
- US-timezone account management is a hard requirement

**Pick Pro Lead Maker if:**

- You want email and LinkedIn run together in one package
- You want a senior team hands-on, not a templated workflow
- Budget efficiency matters (same scope, lower cost)
- You value direct founder access and bespoke copy
